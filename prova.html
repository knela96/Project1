# X-Multiply Arcade Game The game centers around an unusual alien invasion against a colony planet in the year 2249—the aliens themselves are microscopic creatures that invade, infect, and kill the colonists. Scientists have deployed the microscopic fighter X-002 into the body of the hapless woman whose body has been invaded by the alien queen. ## Our Team - Rafel Brau - Management - Pol Cortés - Art & Design - Èric Canela - Code - Ferran Barnés - Code - Eneko Gabaldón - QA
